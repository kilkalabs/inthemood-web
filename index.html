REDISCOVER INTIMACY WITH YOUR PARTNER
In The Mood is the private communication app for couples that strengthens your connection and helps you sync desire, no matter your schedules or different interest levels.

HOW IT WORKS:
- Pair Up: Securely connect with your partner within the app
- Set Your Mood: Privately choose between 'Intimacy', 'Affection', or 'Not in the Mood'
- Get Notified: Receive alerts only when both partners have matching active moods
- Connect Confidently: Eliminate rejection anxiety with our discreet matching system

PERFECT FOR:
- Couples navigating different desire levels
- Busy partners struggling to find moments for connection
- Anyone wanting to improve intimate communication

AVAILABLE MOODS:
- Intimacy: You're feeling ready for close connection
- Affection: You desire closeness, cuddling, or gentle touch
- Not in the Mood: You're not currently receptive

IN THE MOOD+ SUBSCRIPTION:
- Unlock 'Affection' Mood: Expand your ways to signal connection
- Extended Mood Timers: Set your mood to last longer than the standard 3 hours

Stop guessing and start connecting. Download In The Mood today and bring more understanding and intimacy back into your relationship.
